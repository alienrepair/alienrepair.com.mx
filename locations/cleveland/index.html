      About Us

      Computer Repair Doctor of Cleveland, OH opened its doors for business in August of 2017 in University Heights. Its franchise owner, Alex Marini Garvin, a Cleveland native, began working with the company in mid 2015 in Tallahassee Florida as Regional Manager & Senior Technician.

      In early 2017, Alex began the process of moving his life from Florida back home to Ohio with the plan to bring CRD with him. After securing a storefront location on the corner of Cedar & Green rd. in University Heights, he knew he had found a place that felt like home. With tremendous help from family, friends and co-workers, Alex was able to get the store fully remodeled and open within 16 days of moving in!

      We are now proud to say we're ready and waiting to repair greater Cleveland's iPhone/Smartphone, Mac or PC devices!
