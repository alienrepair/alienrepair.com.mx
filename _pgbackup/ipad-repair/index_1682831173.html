    Repair Description

    iPads have gotten very popular - and you probably didn't realize there were so many different versions out already! They're useful for traveling, entertaining your kids, or just browsing on your couch at home if you don't feel like using a laptop. 

    That versatility is a blessing and a curse, because those are situations where it's pretty easy to crack your screen or spill your drink onto your iPad. That's where we come in! iPads are essentially just giant iPhones, and they can break in very similar ways! We're constantly replacing screens and batteries, as well as performing home button repairs, liquid damage cleaning, and more! Check out iPhone Repair or Mac Repair also.

    We're here to help, so if you have any questions about the repair service or maybe just finding out what's wrong with your iPad, feel free to fill out our quote form, give us a call, or stop by during open hours.
